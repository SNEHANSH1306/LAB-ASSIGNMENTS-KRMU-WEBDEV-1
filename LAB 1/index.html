  • MENU MATE:

    This application is designed to simplify weekly meal planning. Users can upload a weekly menu in PDF or JPEG format, and our smart AI system automatically processes the file to generate a clear, day-by-day meal layout. In addition to displaying what's being cooked each day, the app also offers alternative recipe suggestions—perfect for those moments when you're craving something different or want to try a new dish.
    Whether you're managing a household kitchen, a cafeteria, or just organizing your personal meals, this app makes planning and cooking more efficient and enjoyable.
